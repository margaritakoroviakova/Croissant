Croissant🥐

So, what is that?.. 🤔

A croissant is bakery product that contains dough/roll-in fat layers to create a flaky, crispy texture.

    Interesting facts
  • The original croissant was called Kipfel and originated in Austria in 1683.
  • The Croissant became the French national product in 1920.
  • The Croissant started as a luxury product.
  • National Croissant Day is celebrated on the 30th of January.
